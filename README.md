# 🏆 TalentHunt AI – Discovering India's Next Sports Champion

## 🚀 Problem Statement

**SIH25073 – AI-Powered Mobile Platform for Democratizing Sports Talent Assessment**

Millions of talented athletes in India never get recognized because they lack access to professional coaches, scouts, and assessment infrastructure.

**TalentHunt AI** bridges this gap by using Artificial Intelligence to analyze athlete performance, generate talent scores, identify strengths & weaknesses, and provide data-driven sports insights.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To create a future where every athlete, regardless of location or background, gets an equal opportunity to showcase talent and receive professional-level performance assessment.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

### 🤖 AI Talent Assessment

Analyze athlete performance through structured assessment workflows and generate intelligent performance scores.

### 📊 Performance Analytics

Visual dashboards showing:

* Overall Talent Score
* Strength Analysis
* Agility Metrics
* Endurance Performance
* Skill Evaluation

### 🏅 Smart Leaderboard

Compare athletes across multiple sports and identify top performers.

### 👤 Athlete Profiles

Maintain athlete records, performance history, achievements, and assessment reports.

### 📈 Talent Identification Engine

Highlight high-potential athletes who may become future champions.

## 🌍 Impact

TalentHunt AI enables early talent discovery at scale and helps build a more inclusive and data-driven sports ecosystem.

By making sports assessment accessible to everyone, the platform can help identify future athletes from villages, towns, schools, academies, and underserved regions across India.
